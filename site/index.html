Research triage from official public records: not legal advice, proof of intent, or a determination of county authority.

How the radar works

Grounded signals, transparent uncertainty.

01

Discover

Official county sites and linked meeting platforms are resolved, then monitored for agendas, minutes, packets, ordinances, notices, and transcripts.

02

Extract

Documents are content-hashed, versioned, and converted to text. Scanned PDFs use OCR. Unchanged records are never reprocessed.

03

Ground

A high-recall local filter finds regulatory passages. Free LLMs classify only those excerpts; every result must map back to an exact source quote.

04

Score

Risk reflects posture, procedural stage, mechanism, confidence, and recency. Adoption/enforcement persists longer; rescinded actions decay quickly.

What “risk” means here

A prioritized research signal that official records merit review. It is not a prediction, legal conclusion, or assertion of secret conduct.

 Monitoring integrity

County coverage audit

Coverage measures whether the official site was resolved, substantive sources were discovered, sources recently responded, and documents were successfully extracted.
